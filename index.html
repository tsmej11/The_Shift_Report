What we are.

The Shift Report is an independent newsletter produced by rank-and-file RN members of CWA Local 1104 at NewYork-Presbyterian Lower Manhattan Hospital.

We are not the hospital's communications office. We are not the official voice of the Local. We are nurses, writing for nurses, about what's happening on our floors and at our table.

Every member is invited to contribute — story tips, advice column questions, Nurse of the Month nominations, photographs, op-eds. Submissions are reviewed for accuracy and edited for length, not for ideology.

What we stand on.

  1. Members deserve to know what's happening before management announces it.
  2. The contract is only as strong as the members who know how to enforce it.
  3. Safe staffing isn't a benefit — it's the floor, not the ceiling.
  4. Wins should be celebrated publicly. Losses should be learned from publicly.
  5. An injury to one is an injury to all.
